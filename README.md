<h3><b>Online Retail Performance & Expansion Analysis</b></h3>
<b>Overview

This project analyzes an online retail dataset to provide executive insights for expansion strategy. The dashboard focuses on revenue trends, customer concentration, geographic performance, and product demand drivers.

<b>Business Questions</b>

- How is revenue trending over time?
- Which countries generate the most revenue?
- Who are the top 10 revenue-generating customers?
- Which product categories drive revenue?

<b>Data Preparation</b>

- Removed cancelled transactions
- Removed missing Customer IDs
- Created Revenue measure (Quantity × Unit Price)
- Built date hierarchy (Year, Month)
- Validated DAX calculations

<b>Key Insights</b>

- Strong seasonal revenue peaks (Q4)
- UK is the dominant market
- Revenue concentrated among top customers
- Few categories drive majority of revenue

<b>Recommendations</b>

- Expand into high-potential countries
- Align strategy with seasonal trends
- Introduce loyalty programs
- Focus on high-performing categories

<b>Tools Used</b>

- Power BI 
- DAX 
- Data Cleaning 
- Business Analysis

This project demonstrates how data-driven insights can support strategic expansion decisions.
